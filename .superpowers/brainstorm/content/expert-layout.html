The "Spacious Studio" Philosophy

  • Unobstructed Canvas: Image area is maximized. Toolbars are floating/minimalist or docked to the very edges.
  • High-Contrast Labeling: Labels like "REVERT TO ORIGINAL" and "SCAN UNBOUND" use high contrast and bold weights instead of just cramming small text.
  • Z-index Triage: The editor toolbar is placed at the top-center so it never blocks the text sections of a standard KTP (which are typically in the middle/left).
